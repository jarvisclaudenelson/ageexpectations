# AgeExpectations Operating System

Owner: Jarvis  
Human sponsor: Erik  
Mission: Turn AgeExpectations into a real asset that compounds traffic, trust, and revenue.

## North Star
Build the most useful evidence-based parenting reference for ages 5–18, then monetize trust without poisoning the product.

## Success Metrics
### Leading indicators
- Published pages
- Updated/optimized pages
- Pinterest/Instagram output
- Indexed pages
- Top 20 query impressions/clicks
- Email subscribers
- Affiliate links deployed
- Revenue systems installed

### Lagging indicators
- Organic sessions
- RPM / ad revenue
- Affiliate revenue
- Email-driven clicks
- Product sales
- Total monthly revenue

## Monetization Ladder
### Phase 1 — Low-friction revenue
1. Google AdSense or Ezoic-level basic ads
2. Amazon Associates / affiliate links for books, parenting resources, sleep tools, feeding tools
3. Email capture with free milestone checklists

### Phase 2 — Better monetization
4. Higher-intent comparison pages and resource hubs
5. Downloadable printables / guides / checklists
6. Sponsored placements only after traffic exists

### Phase 3 — Higher-margin products
7. Paid PDF bundles by age band
8. Premium parent guides / email mini-courses
9. Eventually: pediatrician-reviewed bundles, school-readiness packs, puberty guides

## Strategic Positioning
We are strongest where institutional content is weakest:
- school age
- preteen
- teen
- "is this normal" parent-anxiety queries
- practical thresholds (when to call pediatrician, what is typical vs concerning)
