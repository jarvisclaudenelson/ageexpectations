# AgeExpectations.com Monetization Plan

## Objective
Generate diversified revenue streams by leveraging high-authority, age-specific parenting content.

## Phase 1: Low Barrier (Launch – 10k Monthly Sessions)

### 1. Granular Affiliate Marketing (Amazon/Target/Walmart)
- **Strategy:** Instead of generic "Best Baby Gear" lists, embed age-specific recommendations directly in the articles.
- **Examples:**
  - 0–1 Month: High-contrast cards, swaddles, Vitamin D drops.
  - 6–9 Months: High chairs, silicone bibs, sippy cups.
- **Implementation:** Automatic "Essential Gear for [Age]" blocks at the bottom of every page.

### 2. Lead Generation for Specialists
- **Strategy:** Partner with remote service providers (Sleep Consultants, Lactation Consultants, Postpartum Doulas).
- **Implementation:** "Book a [Specialist]" buttons in relevant sections (e.g., in the "Sleep" topic for 4-month-olds).

## Phase 2: Authority Building (10k – 50k Monthly Sessions)

### 3. Digital "Survival Guides"
- **Strategy:** Package the web content into high-value, printable PDFs.
- **Product:** "The First 12 Months: A Weekly Survival Guide" or "The Toddler Tantrum Toolkit."
- **Price Point:** $19 – $49.

### 4. Niche Newsletter
- **Strategy:** Capture emails via "Get the [Age] Guide" lead magnets.
- **Implementation:** Weekly emails tailored to the child's exact age with tips and product features.

## Phase 3: Scaling (50k+ Monthly Sessions)

### 5. Programmatic Display Ads
- **Strategy:** Apply for high-tier ad networks (Mediavine or Raptive).
- **Target CPM:** $25 – $40 (Parenting is a high-value niche).

### 6. Data Licensing & AEO Partnerships
- **Strategy:** As the site becomes a "canonical" source for Answer Engines (Perplexity/Gemini), explore partnerships or API access for parenting apps that need structured developmental data.
